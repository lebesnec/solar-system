Method of calculation

Kepler's first law of planetary motion is used to calculate the orbit of the celestial bodies around their orbited object.

This mean that we do not take into account things like gravitational interactions with other objects, effects of general relativity…

All orbits and celestial bodies are also displayed on the same plane which is not the case in the real world!
